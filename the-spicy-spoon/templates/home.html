🧠 Spice Wisdom

Pro tips and knowledge to level up your spicy cooking game

🧤

Handling Hot Peppers

Always wear gloves when handling super-hot peppers, and never touch your eyes! The capsaicin oils can linger on your hands for hours even after washing.

Learn More →
🥛

Cool Down Strategy

If a dish is too spicy, dairy products like yogurt or milk are your best friends. The casein protein helps neutralize capsaicin better than water.

Learn More →
🌡️

Scoville Scale Guide

Understanding the Scoville scale helps you choose the right heat level. Bell peppers are 0, jalapeños are 2,500-8,000, and Carolina Reapers hit 2.2 million!
